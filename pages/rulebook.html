RULEBOOK

1. TOURNAMENT SYSTEM

This system divides the competition into two phases. The first phase consists of a league where they face everyone against everyone for 5 or 7 weeks. The second phase is eliminatory, whose number of rounds will limit the total number of teams that continue to play after the first phase.
The highest ranked teams will go to the playoffs where they will face each other in a certain order and after each match, a losing team will be eliminated and the winning team will advance to the next round.

2.PUNCTUATION SYSTEM

2.1. LEAGUE

  • Win: 3 Points
  • Tie (stars and percentage): 1 Point
  • Loss: 0 Points

    • 2.2. CLASSIFICATION

  • Points
  • Difference of stars (Stars in favor - Stars against)
  • Average percentage of destruction

    • If a team leaves the tournament, victory will be given by default in the remaining weeks.

    2.3. PLAYOFFS

    CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID: The playoffs of these divisions will be defined in a Bo2 format (best of 2 wars). In exceptional cases, if they continue to be tied in terms of stars and percentage, a third war will be played.

    HEAVY: In cases of a tie in this division, the clan that has made the fewest attacks at the end of the war will be considered as the winner. If the tie persists, a "Sudden Death" war will be used according to the same detailed format for the Express and Diamonds division.

    DIAMONDS AND EXPRESS: In the event of a tie in stars (At this stage the destruction percentage is obsolete) a sudden death war will take place for the tiebreaker. This war will be played immediately as detailed in "Sudden Death".

    2.4. SUDDEN DEATH

  • 5v5 format
  • BreakDown 2/2/1 - (Diamonds 0/0/0/2/2/1)
  • Attacks per account: 1
  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • War duration: 30 minutes

    • Challenge the clan that appears in the published pact of the initial war, there will be a maximum of 10 minutes to send the challenge and 5 minutes for the rival clan to accept the war; if this is not done, the corresponding clan will be victorious by default.

    3. REGULATION

    3.1. TROOPS, SPELLS AND SIEGES

    Troops, spells, and temporary sieges will not be allowed in all divisions.

    Heavy, Express: In these divisions the use of the following "witch and super witch" troops will be regulated by limiting the amount of their use per attack, the maximum amount of said troop per attack is specified below:
  • 5 Witches
  • 2 Super Witches

    • Note: Donations from the clan castle are taken into account in the sum.

    The troop levels for both attacks and defenses can be of any level.

    Masters, Gladiators, Hybrid, Diamonds: Troops of the same town hall level will only be allowed in both defense and attack, example: a TH11 can only receive donations from another TH11.

    NOTE: For sudden death in Diamonds due to BD being 0/0/0/2/2/1 a TH10 will be allowed to donate on defense as an attack to a TH9; but you can only donate troops that correspond to be unlocked in TH9

    4. TRANFERS

    Transfers can be made anytime up to 30 minutes prior to the war challenge submission.
  • HEAVY, EXPRESS, DIAMONDS (15 signings)
  • CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID (5 signings)

    "If the local time of a country changes, it will be the responsibility of the representatives to announce it in advance."

    The duration of the preparation and the day of the battle will be the following for each division:

    CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID: 5 minutes of preparation and 1 hour of the day of the battle.

    • 7. SANCTIONS


    INFRINGEMENT SANCTION DETAIL
    Entry and exit of accounts. -1 star The entry and exit of an account is allowed during war preparation, once the war has started, the entry of accounts is prohibited.
    The accounts that do not participate in the war can leave without problem, but remember that they cannot enter again.
    The accounts that participate in war can only exit after having made their "2 attacks or 1 attack depending on their division", otherwise they will be sanctioned.
    DIP or SCOUT attack -1 star The attack will be canceled and will not add stars and percentage, remember that they will have to attack this village again to recover the stars and percentage.
    It will not be penalized if the attack ends in 0%.
    Off-roster account -1 star It will only be penalized if the account attacks.
    The account outside ROSTER will not be able to attack but it must be attacked normally.
    Alter the BreakDown No star or point discount. In case of altering the BreakDown, you must speak with the representative of the other clan to match the BreakDown, example: Team A - BD 7/8/10 Team B - BD 7/7/11 Solution: Team A - A Rh13 must not attack. Team B - Rh12 must not attack
    Delay in Send / Accept the war challenge The second SLIGHT offense will be awarded a victory by default.
    The first SERIOUS infraction will be awarded a victory by default.
    NOTE: The stars and percentages for these victories are detailed in the table "WINS BY DEFAULT"    		 CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID: MINOR offense: exceed 5 MIN. 59 SEC. SERIOUS infraction: exceed 10 MIN. 59 SEC. HEAVY, EXPRESS, DIAMONDS: MINOR offense: exceed 10 MIN. 59 SEC. SERIOUS infraction: exceed minute 15:59.
    Breach the Troop Regulation CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID: -1 Star for each offending attack HEAVY, EXPRESS, DIAMONDS: The attack will be counted as invalid, no stars will be deducted.
    Another player will be able to attack again to recover the stars.    		 Applies for attacks with banned troops or for attacks exceeding the limit of the troop regulation.
    In the divisions CHAMPS, MASTERS, GLADIATORS, HYBRID, DIAMONDS: applies to attacks with troops of a level NOT suitable for each town hall.
    Inappropriate behaviors Possible expulsion depending on severity. All clans and their members must maintain respectful behavior through social media, clan description, village designs, spell drawings, or any other behavior that may be considered inappropriate / offensive.
    Use of third party applications Expulsion from the tournament Any use of third party applications is prohibited.
    Any evidence of the use of such programs by the participating clans will be enough to expel the clan from the tournament.
